Manifesto

We are Pax Unitatis , a neutral order dedicated to maintaining balance in a divided world. We stand for diplomacy, progress, and shared prosperity. In times of discord, we remain the constant — an anchor of reason, generosity, and unwavering neutrality. We do not take sides. We build bridges through cooperation and uphold the idea that strength lies not in dominance, but in unity.

Through advanced automation, shared resources, and transparent negotiation, we empower all factions to thrive without fear or prejudice. We are not a faction — we are the foundation upon which others can coexist.

The Accord of War

The Accord of War is a multilateral treaty enforced by Pax Unitatis to ensure that all armed conflict on the server remains honorable, constructive, and respectful to neutral grounds and players. Violations of this accord may lead to sanctions or removal from shared services provided by Pax Unitatis.

Article I – Declaration of Conflict

  • Wars must be declared publicly at least 6 hours in advance.
  • Surprise attacks or offline raiding is prohibited.
  • Neutral grounds under Pax Unitatis are off-limits for combat.

Article II – Combat Conduct

  • Combat must be consensual and not involve spawn camping.
  • Players must not destroy civilian infrastructure or unrelated builds.

Article III – Raiding Rules

  • Raiding is allowed only after declaration of war.
  • Only military targets may be looted; personal and decorative builds are protected.

Article IV – Banned Tactics

  • No lava spam or mass TNT carpet bombing in civilian zones.
  • No portal traps or invisibility potions during raids.
  • No excessive mob spamming to lag the server.

Article V – Special Restrictions

  • Dikče is permanently banned from using Netherite gear.

Article VI – Peace & Mediation

  • Pax Unitatis may propose peace or ceasefire at any time.
  • All sides must attend a summit to finalize peace after hostilities end.

Article VII – Consequences for Violation

  • Violators may be censured publicly or lose access to shared Pax Unitatis resources.
  • Severe cases may lead to full ostracization from the neutral network.
